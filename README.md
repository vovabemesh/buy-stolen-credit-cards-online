## Protecting Yourself from Credit Card Fraud
Rather than engaging, focus on security:
- **Monitor Accounts Regularly**: Use apps for real-time alerts on suspicious transactions.
- **Enable Two-Factor Authentication**: Add layers to your banking logins.
- **Use Virtual Cards**: For online shopping, opt for one-time-use numbers.
- **Freeze Credit Reports**: In case of suspected breaches, lock your credit files.
